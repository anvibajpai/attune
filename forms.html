Birth Certificate

Under the law, you have 30 days from the moment the school requests for a copy of your child's birth certificate or some other proof of identity, such as a passport.

Report Card or Transcript

Under the law, the school should request a copy of your child's school records from the last school they attended. The school has 10 days to provide this copy, or you have 30 days to provide the records instead.

Immunization Record

If your child has had all their vaccines, then you must provide a copy of your child's immunization records for health and safety reasons. These copies can be obtained through contacting the child's primary care physician. There are exceptions if your child is waiting to complete immunizations.

Proof of Residency

Gather documents to show that you live within the boundaries of the school. Your name must be on the lease, utility bill, or other document connected to the residence where you are staying. If your name is not on these documents because you are staying with a friend or immediate relative, you are entitled to immediate enrollment without needing these documents as you are McKinney Vento eligible (a law).
